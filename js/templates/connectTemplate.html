Connect

At Mosaic we believe that the church should be a place where you are challenged and at the same time accepted regardless of what you believe, or what culture you come from or background you have. We also believe that people connect in different ways and at different levels. So at Mosaic there are 3 ways you can connect with us: In our homes at one of our Missional Communities (small groups), out in everyday life & at our Sunday morning services 10:30am @ Grahamwood Elementary School, 3950 Summer Ave Memphis, TN 38122. You can also connect with us through Facebook, Twitter or by signing up to our email list.
