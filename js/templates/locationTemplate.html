Location

Our service is at Grahamwood Elementary School at 10:30am. Our desire is to bring the gospel into a new urban environment in a language they can comprehend and with a cultural form they can connect with. Mosaic Church will be that kind of church. We believe God is calling us to be a church of many nations, cultures, and socio-economic backgrounds. And we believe God has called us to embrace, love, and renew the city.
