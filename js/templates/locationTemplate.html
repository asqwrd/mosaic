Location

We are currently having weekly meetings on Thursdays at the location above. We are raising support to begin meeting at Grahamwood School in the fall. This community needs a church that will attempt to bring the gospel into a new urban environment in a language they can comprehend and in a cultural form they can connect with. Mosaic Church will be that kind of church. We believe God is calling us to be a church of many nations, cultures, and socio-economic backgrounds. And we believe God has called us to embrace, love, and renew the city. So we choose this area because it is de-churched, diverse and in the city.
